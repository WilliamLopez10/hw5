Dogs

Canis lupus familiaris

Dogs are domesticated animals that can make for a great house pet. Dogs are known to be smart, friendly, and nice. A dogs life span is, on average, between 10 to 13 years. Dogs come in many different breeds that are all different shapes, sizes, color, and even behavior. Some breeds are known to be more mischeaviours than others, like the shiba, while dogs like the chihuahua are known the be a little fiesty. Overall I think dogs are cute and gentle creatures that are loved by everyone.
